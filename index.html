About Us

DA-IICT is a premier engineering institute in Gujarat, set with the objectives of imparting higher education, research, and training in various fields of engineering & technology. What sets DA-IICT apart is its unconventional academic approach. Students are encouraged right from the start to step outside of the textbook world, interact with like-minded people and think about real world problems with the viewpoint of finding a solution. DA-IICT has emphasized and encouraged research right from its founding in the year 2001. A fundamental principle followed at DA-IICT from day one is that teaching and research must remain equally vital and important elements in shaping of an academic institution.

A hackathon is a sprint-like problem-focused computer programming event, as well as pitch, program, and present instances of prototype digital innovation (e.g. a prototype mobile application). They bring together programmers, interface designers, graphic designers and others to collaborate intensively over a short period of time on software projects. These hackathons encourage experimentation and creativity, and can be challenge orientated.
